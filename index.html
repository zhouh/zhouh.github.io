Email: zhouhao.nlpbytedance.com , haozhou0806gmail.com

Hello! I am a researcher at Bytedance AI lab, working on natural language processing. My research interests include syntax parsing, neural machine translation and natural language generation.

Before that, I was a PhD candidate in the NLP group of Nanjing University, advised by Prof. Jiajun Chen. I have also been a visiting student in Singapore University of Technology and Design, working with Prof. Yue Zhang, and a research intern in Huawei Noah’s Ark Lab, Hong Kong, with Dr. Zhaopeng Tu.
